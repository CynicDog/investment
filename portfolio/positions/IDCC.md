## Thesis

Quality cash machine with derisked top-3 customers. InterDigital licenses wireless / video standards-essential patents (cellular, Wi-Fi, HEVC/AV1). The major customer concentration that historically suppressed the multiple has been worked through via signed long-term agreements, removing the binary "will they renew" overhang on the largest counterparties. What remains is a high-margin, capital-light, royalty-generating business returning meaningful cash via buybacks and a growing dividend.

## Bull case

- Top-3 customer renewals already signed — primary multi-year overhang resolved.
- Royalty stream is recurring with high incremental margins.
- Aggressive buybacks at low multiples.
- Smaller-cap Asian licensee whitespace remains a multi-year tail.

## Bear case & risks

- Licensing revenue is lumpy by quarter — print volatility creates noise.
- Patent litigation overhang in some jurisdictions.
- Multiple compression if a major customer disputes scope at renewal.
- Smaller-cap, less institutional sponsorship — illiquidity in stress.
