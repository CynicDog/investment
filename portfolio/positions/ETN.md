## Thesis

AI power infrastructure. Eaton's electrical-power business sits at the center of the hyperscale datacenter buildout — switchgear, busway, UPS, and power-distribution gear are all gating items for AI-capacity expansion. Grid modernization, renewables interconnection, and EV charging infra are additional multi-year capex drivers; aerospace and vehicle segments add diversification. Pricing power has shown through margins. Held for the cycle, not for a re-rating.

## Bull case

- Datacenter power demand is structural and accelerating.
- Grid spend in the US/EU is multi-decade, with backlog visibility.
- Margin expansion track record through several cycles.
- Disciplined capital returns: dividend + buyback.

## Bear case & risks

- Cyclical end-markets — a true industrial recession compresses orders.
- Multiple has expanded materially; no longer the cheap-quality name it was.
- Execution risk on backlog conversion; supply-chain frictions.
- Concentrated end-market exposure if datacenter capex pauses.
