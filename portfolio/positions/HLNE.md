- Recurring fee revenue dominates; carry is upside, not the base case.
- High insider ownership; aligned management.
- Operating leverage as platform scales — incremental margins are very high.

## Bear case & risks

- Key-person concentration in an advisory business.
- Multiple compression if rates stay structurally higher and private-market returns disappoint vs. promises.
- Fee compression risk as evergreen / retail products proliferate.
