## Description

Johnson Fistel, PLLP announced an investor investigation on April 28, 2026 (GlobeNewsWire: https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/04/28/3283318/0/en/hamilton-lane-incorporated-shareholders-are-encouraged-to-reach-out-to-johnson-fistel-for-more-information-about-potentially-recovering-their-losses.html), recruiting shareholders for a potential securities class-action arising from the same Hunterbrook allegations (FRE methodology, day-one markups). No complaint has been filed as of 2026-05-15. This is distinct from R-2026-05-001 (which covers the short report itself): litigation risk introduces costs, discovery obligations, management distraction, and potential class-action liability even if the underlying accounting allegations prove baseless.

## Monitor for

Formal class-action complaint filing against HLNE; any SEC inquiry triggered by the law-firm investigations; management commentary on litigation reserve or legal costs on the May 21 call; second plaintiff firm joining or consolidating action.
