## Description

Samsung has filed a challenge to the $1.05B arbitration decision awarded to InterDigital. If overturned or materially reduced, this would directly impact near-term revenue recognition and could weaken IDCC's negotiating leverage with other unlicensed parties (Hisense, TCL). Per IAM Media (2026), the challenge was active as of Q1 2026 close with no resolution disclosed in the Q1 earnings press release.

## Monitor for

Arbitration appeal rulings or court decisions in the Samsung dispute; any settlement announcement; management commentary on Samsung receivable recognition; read-through to Hisense/TCL negotiating posture.
