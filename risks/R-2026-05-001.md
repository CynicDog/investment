## Description

Hunterbrook Media published a short report on Apr 27, 2026 alleging: (1) ~1/3 of Hamilton Lane's Private Assets Fund gains are attributable to 'day-one markups' on secondary assets; (2) FRE margin inflated from ~32% to ~59% by switching to NAV-based carry and excluding stock-based compensation; (3) without these accounting choices, FRE would have declined 16% vs. the reported 37% growth over 9 months ended Dec 2025; (4) one evergreen fund saw ~$172M net outflow in March 2026. HLNE dismissed allegations and announced legal recourse. Stock fell 5.67% on Apr 28 to $89.46 (source: Benzinga Apr 28, 2026 https://www.benzinga.com/trading-ideas/movers/26/04/52078786/hamilton-lane-shares-slide-following-hunterbrook-short-report; Hunterbrook report https://hntrbrk.com/hamilton-lane/).

## Monitor for

May 21 Q4 FY2026 earnings call: management response to Hunterbrook allegations, explicit FRE margin methodology disclosure, and Q4 Evergreen platform net flow data; any SEC or regulatory inquiry announcement; independent forensic accounting review or restatement; sustained Evergreen outflow trends in subsequent quarters.
