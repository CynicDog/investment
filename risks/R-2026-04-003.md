## Description

The April 7, 2026 Vertex Pharmaceuticals Hypercon deal ($15M upfront, milestones, royalties on up to 3 targets) is the most recent Hypercon commercial partnership. Total Hypercon licensees number four: Vertex, Janssen, Eli Lilly, argenx (PR Newswire, Apr 7). Near-term Hypercon royalty ramp depends on execution by this small counterparty set. ENHANZE royalties ($1.13-1.17B 2026 guidance) remain the dominant income source and are not affected.
