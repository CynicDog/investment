## Description

Management disclosed on the April 29, 2026 Q1 2026 earnings call a 'current shortfall in collateral against our total exposure' in State National's fronting operations with a capacity provider, triggered by an upward revision in loss ratio estimates after a claims trend review. No dollar amount disclosed. Management characterized the shortfall as not material to earnings or capital, and stated contractual recourse is being pursued and an outside actuarial review has been engaged. Materiality is management's assertion only; no independent validation is publicly available (Investing.com Q1 transcript, Apr 29 2026: https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-markel-group-q1-2026-results-disappoint-with-major-eps-miss-93CH-4645999).

## Monitor for

Q2 2026 earnings call (est. late July) for resolution update or dollar disclosure; any 8-K or 10-Q amendment disclosing revised materiality assessment; outside actuarial review conclusions; whether capacity provider dispute escalates to litigation or loss-sharing adjustment.
